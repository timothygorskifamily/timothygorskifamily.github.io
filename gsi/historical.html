Methodology Note

This tool is a theoretical prototype used for demonstrative purposes. Historical performance data is simulated based on retrospective application of the GSI strategy mechanics (Credit Yield + Levered Options) against historical market conditions. Backtested/simulated performance returns are hypothetical and do not reflect trading in actual accounts.

Data Sources & Assumptions

  • S&P 500: NYU Stern / Yahoo Finance (Total Return Data).
  • Private Equity (Net): Cambridge Associates. Modeled here as Gross Return less 1.5% Mgmt Fee & 20% Carry (High Water Mark).
  • Credit Base: Fed Funds Rate + 600bps Spread (Simulated).
  • Bonds: Bloomberg US Aggregate Bond Index.
