About the Gorski Foundation

Who We Are

The Gorski Foundation is a Christian philanthropic organization run by the Gorski Family Office. Our focus is on supporting evangelical organizations both in the United States and abroad. We believe in the power of faith-based initiatives to transform lives and communities, and we are committed to providing resources, encouragement, and support to those on the frontlines.

Our Story

Founded in response to a calling to serve, the Gorski Foundation has grown from a local family effort into a global partner for evangelical missions. Our journey is marked by a deep commitment to Christian values, transparency, and impactful giving.
