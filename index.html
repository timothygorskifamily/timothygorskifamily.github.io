Gorski Family Office

The Gorski Family Office is a private investment firm specializing in institutional private equity, hedge funds, and structured investments. We partner with leading global asset managers such as Hamilton Lane, Blackstone, Ares, KKR, Apollo, Blackrock, North Haven, Veritas, Clearlake, Alphakeys, Churchill, and Coller to drive long-term growth and stewardship.

Gorski Foundation

The Gorski Foundation is a Christian philanthropic organization supporting evangelical groups and community programs locally and globally. Our mission includes uplifting vulnerable women in Latin America, supporting immigrant communities, advancing global health education, urban youth programs, and missionary work.
