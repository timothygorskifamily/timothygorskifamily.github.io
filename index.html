Gorski Foundation

Run by the Gorski Family Office, the Gorski Foundation is a Christian foundation dedicated to supporting evangelical organizations that make a meaningful impact in their communities, both domestically and internationally.

Our Mission

To empower and support evangelical organizations that embody Christian values and provide hope, relief, and opportunity to those they serve. Through strategic giving, we aim to strengthen faith-based initiatives and help communities flourish worldwide.

What We Support

  • Local and global evangelical ministries
  • Community outreach and relief programs
  • Missionary work and church planting projects
  • Christian education and youth initiatives
  • Faith-based humanitarian and disaster response efforts
