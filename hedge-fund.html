Hedge Fund Strategies

Disciplined Risk Management and Downside Protection

Strategic Overview

The Gorski Family Office operates a dedicated hedge fund strategy engineered to navigate complex market environments. Our core focus centers on long-dated derivative trading and comprehensive downside protection for public indices.

Derivative Trading

By utilizing long-dated derivatives, we position our capital to capture macro trends and asymmetric payoffs. This approach allows us to maintain targeted market exposure while optimizing capital efficiency and mitigating short-term volatility.

Downside Protection

Capital preservation is paramount. Our quantitative approach to hedging public indices acts as a structural shock absorber, ensuring the broader portfolio remains insulated during periods of severe market dislocation or systemic stress.
