Featured Initiatives

International & Medical Missions

Through partners like Operation Mobilization International and International Help, we deploy critical resources and health education to underserved regions. Our global footprint extends to supporting educational development through organizations like We are Kenya.

Local Church & Community

We believe transformation starts at home. We actively support Calvary Chapel Ellicott City in their local ministry, alongside Iglesia Conexion al Reino to uplift and provide essential support to immigrant communities navigating new environments.

Youth & Women's Empowerment

Protecting the vulnerable is central to our mission. We champion urban youth transformation through Teen Blast and partner closely with Be Love to restore dignity, provide safe havens, and empower vulnerable women across Latin America.
