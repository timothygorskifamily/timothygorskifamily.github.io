Grants & Programs

How We Support

The Gorski Foundation offers grants and program support to evangelical organizations. Our focus is on projects that deliver measurable impact and align with our Christian values.

Application Process

If you represent an organization seeking support, please review our eligibility criteria and submit a proposal using our contact form.

  • Eligibility: Evangelical Christian organizations, domestic or international
  • Focus: Community support, faith-based outreach, humanitarian aid
  • Review: All applications are prayerfully and thoughtfully considered
