About Us

The Gorski Foundation is a Christian foundation focused on supporting evangelical domestic and international organizations that transform communities through faith and service.

Who We Support

  • Calvary Chapel Of Ellicott City – Local church ministry
  • Be Love – Restoring dignity and empowering vulnerable women in Latin America
  • Iglesia Conexion al Reino – Supporting the immigrant community
  • International Help – Overseas health education
  • Mexico Broadcast Ministry – Media outreach
  • TeenBlast – Urban youth program
  • Missionary Work – Sweden and Polish Baptist Association
  • ...and many other faith-based non-profits
