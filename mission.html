Our Mission

Supporting Evangelical Organizations

The mission of the Gorski Foundation is to empower evangelical organizations that are making a difference in their communities. We provide funding, resources, and encouragement to Christian leaders and organizations committed to spreading the Gospel and serving those in need.

Areas of Focus

  • Faith-based education and outreach
  • Community development and support
  • International missions and humanitarian aid
  • Leadership development for Christian organizations
